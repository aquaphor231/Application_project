Hi! I'm Blessing.

I'm a future software engineer.

I am from Ghana where I lived from the ages 4 to 8 but was raised in Philadelphia. I love Ghanaian food! My favorite is banku with okra stew but waakye is a close second.

I've had the opportunity to explore new activities like going into the outdoors in my time at Pomona College. I went hiking and skiing for the first time! I have also tried boxing and West African Dance.

I recently started crocheting. Take a look at
one of my creations (just a really, really long purple scarf) :)

I began computer science last spring and sought community, so I joined Black and Brown in Computer Science where I am now one of the three coordinators/presidents of the cohort. I facilitate mentorship, community events like boba study nights and speaker events, communicate with the computer science department about student needs, attend leadership meetings, etc. to provide the community I found solace in to others.

Last summer, I received the opportunity to participate in a program called Pathways to AI at NYU where I learned about what research in computer science looks like. I was introduced to NLP, robotics, computer vision, and more. Now I want to learn more interesting applications.

I also met some cool people and got to play with robots!
